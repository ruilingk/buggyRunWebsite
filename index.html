Who are we?
2 passionate programmers who knew each other since JC and want to create a game for everyone!

Why are we called Linged-list?
Our names are Wen Ling and Rui Ling, so we thought it would be cool if we extract out the "ling"s and create a group name based on it. In addition, with the data structure Linked-list, we thought it would be funny if we called ourselves linged-list. :)

Say goodbye to your normal Mario-style brick obstacles and say hello to arrays, BSTs and even linked-lists ;)! buggyRun's obstacles are in the form of data structures, which are relatable to programmers.
